Who is Ruth?

Ruth 1

The book of Ruth in the old testament is centred around a woman called Ruth and her mother-in-law Naomi. Ruth was a Moabite, and was around in the time when the Judges ruled.

*Around 1200 - 1020 b.c. This time was hard and desperate for everyone. There were famines and wars, it was a difficult period to live through.


The story of Ruth has a few main ideas. 1. Ruth's loyalty to Naomi throughout the book and 2. That the book never mentions God.

When both of Naomi's sons died, Ruth declared that she would go with Naomi back to her homeland in Judah - even though Naomi wanted to go alone.

So the story begins with Naomi and Ruth returning back to Judah, both widowed with no males to look after them. They were refugees.

Ruth 1 : 16 - 17
But Ruth replied, “Don't urge me to leave you or to turn back from you. Where you go I will go, and where you stay I will stay. Your people will be my people and your God my God.”
